FAQ

Rewards
1. How do I get started with referring?
a. Watch the Ambassador 101 info session to learn how to get started as an Ambassador.
2. When will I get my referral reward?
a. Referral rewards are sent via check within 90 days of your referrals enrollment date. If you choose to have your reward applied to your tuition, this will be reflected on your account within 90 days.
My friend enrolled, how do I get my reward?  
a. Firstly, ensure that your friend is listed as enrolled in your Referral Tracker, and that your current address and social security number (if you’re a U.S. citizen) are listed on your personal information page. If this information is present then there is no further action required and you can expect to receive your reward via check within 90 days. If any of these steps are missing, or you have not received your check within 90 days, please contact coachblair@integrativenutrition.com .
4. What form does my reward come in?  
a. You will receive a check to the address provided on your personal information page. If you would like your reward applied to your tuition, or a friend’s tuition, simply email coachblair@integrativenutrition.com. When applying to tuition, the reward will be applied to the overall balance, and will not cover any upcoming tuition payments.
5. Can I apply my reward to my tuition?  
a. Yes! Just email coachblair@integrativenutrition.com with your request. Your reward can be applied to your tuition, or a friends tuition (as long as tuition is not paid in full.) When applying to tuition, it will be applied to the overall balance, and will not cover any upcoming tuition payments.
6. How much money will I receive?   
a. Your reward amount is based on your Ambassador Level. You can see this information on the banner underneath your name on the shareIIN home page. The breakdown is:
  • 1. Bronze Ambassador (0-5 referrals) = $300
  • 2. Silver Ambassadors (6-19 referrals) = $400
  • 3. Gold Ambassadors (20-49 referrals) = $600
  • 4. Platinum Ambassadors (50+ referrals) = $1000
 7. How do I check to see if I’m an Ambassador so I can get my referral reward?  
a. As long as you are signed in to shareIIN.com and you are receiving emails from Coach Blair, you are an Ambassador! If you have any questions regarding this, feel free to email coachblair@integrativenutrition.com.
Tracking Your Referral
8. I don't see my friend listed on my referral; can you add her?  
a. Always double check your referral tracker for your friends name. If you do not see this reflected, email coachblair@integrativenutrition.com with your friends name and email address so we can connect you.
9. Where is my tracker?  
a. You can find your Referral Tracker here. You can also find this by clicking the drop down menu located next to your photo at the top of the shareIIN.com home page.
Referring
10. How do I refer my friend?
a. There are several ways to refer, you can read more about them here.
11. How can I add my friend to my tracker?  
a. You cannot manually add referrals to your tracker. Please introduce your friends via one of these methods.
12. How do I add a banner to my website?  
a. Please refer to the step by step guide, and/or the step by step video.
13. How do I add a banner to my website?  
a. Only banner links can be posted to Facebook and be tracked to you. Copy and paste the banner link of your choice onto your Facebook page, and anyone who clicks this will be listed as your referral.
14. How can I share a link on Facebook and have it tracked to me?  
a. You can change your email address by updating your personal information on your Student Dashboard.
General/Misc
15. Are there any savings offers available right now?  
a. As an Ambassador, you have exclusive access to the Ambassador Savings Offer to share at any time. Additional savings opportunities will be shared via email from Coach Blair, on the Ambassador Facebook group, and on the shareIIN blog.
